DAKSH CHADHA


About

Daksh Chadha is a student from the EICAR film school Paris. He has worked on a number of short films as a writer, director, cinematographer and assistant cinematographer. He started his career with photography and eventually got into film making. Having featured in a number of magazines and newspapers (Sanctuary Asia and Times of India), he has also been featured on the 'MTV wall of fame'.

His films have been selected and national and international film festivals in India and Europe. He has worked as an assistant to DOP Ravi Varman for the films 'Jagga Jasoos' and 'Sanjay Dutt Biopic'. He recently started his own production house called 'Basholi Films'. He has currently been making short films, commercial ad films and music videos.
